Brewery

In order to recreate the traditional technological methods and taste characteristics of beer, the plant underwent major renovations and began work in 2011. Copper varnas were reconstructed, the fermentation department and cellars were redone, the most sophisticated filtration and bottling systems were installed, allowing to bring all beer lovers the true value and taste of our beer.

„Podkováň - beer with a traditional flavor“.
